=========================================================
PLGA NANOPARTICLE DRUG DELIVERY SIMULATION
PARAMETER IMPACT GUIDE
=========================================================

This document describes the role of each model parameter,
its effect on the simulation, and which results are most
strongly influenced.

2. GRID RESOLUTION
=========================================================

Nx = Grid Points (X)

Ny = Grid Points (Y)

• Better gradients

Decrease

• Lower spatial resolution

Affects

All Results
