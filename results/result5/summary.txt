Sensitivity Analysis
====================

Parameters were varied by 80%, 100%, and 120%.
Tumor reduction was calculated directly from simulated tumor volumes.

Drug Release Rate: baseline=101259.04, modified=79130.35, reduction=21.85%
Drug Uptake Rate: baseline=101259.04, modified=94755.00, reduction=6.42%
Tumor Growth Rate: baseline=101259.04, modified=62729.73, reduction=38.05%
Drug Efficacy: baseline=101259.04, modified=50000.00, reduction=50.62%

Interpretation:
Highest influence parameter: Drug Efficacy.
Lowest influence parameter: Drug Uptake.

The tornado chart ranks parameters by the relative change in final tumor volume caused by parameter variation.
The spider plot uses fixed normalized sensitivity scaling from 0 to 1.
The tumor reduction chart compares the best achievable reduction for each parameter.
